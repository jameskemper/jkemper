Welcome!

I'm a new lecturer in the Department of Economics at Texas Tech University. Before joining Texas Tech, I was an Assistant Professor of Economics at South Plains College for eleven years. Prior to academia, I was an investment banker right during the Great Recession.

I'm passionate about teaching economics and am excited to be a part of such an innovative and research-focused department. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions! Wreck'em!
